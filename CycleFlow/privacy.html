CycleFlow - Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 6, 2026

Introduction

CycleFlow ("the App") is a period and cycle tracking application developed by Ruslan Lutsenko. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection & Storage

All cycle data, symptoms, and personal information you enter into the App is stored locally on your device and optionally synced via your personal iCloud account. We do not collect, transmit, or store your data on any external servers.

HealthKit Integration

CycleFlow may integrate with Apple HealthKit to read and write cycle-related health data. This data is handled in accordance with Apple's HealthKit guidelines and is never shared with third parties or used for advertising.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit your personal data to any third parties. There are no third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools in the App.

Analytics

CycleFlow does not collect any analytics or usage data. We have no mechanism to identify or track individual users.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by removing the App from your device. If you use iCloud sync, you can also delete your data from iCloud via your device settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
