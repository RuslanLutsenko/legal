Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my subscription?

Go to Settings inside the app and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used to subscribe.

Where is my data stored?

All your journal entries are stored locally on your device and optionally synced via your personal iCloud account. We do not have access to your data.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the Settings app on your iPhone → tap your name → Subscriptions → select Chronicle → Cancel Subscription.

Is my journal private?

Yes. Chronicle supports Face ID / Touch ID lock. All data stays on your device and iCloud.

System Requirements

Chronicle requires iOS 17.0 or later. The app is compatible with iPhone.
