DreamTales - Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 6, 2026

Introduction

DreamTales ("the App") is a fairy tale application for children developed by Ruslan Lutsenko. We take children's privacy very seriously. This policy explains how we handle data.

COPPA Compliance

DreamTales is designed to be compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App does not require account creation or any personal data to function.

Data Collection

DreamTales does not collect any personal data from its users. Stories are generated and stored locally on the device. No data is transmitted to external servers.

No Analytics, Ads, or Third-Party SDKs

The App contains no analytics tools, no advertising, and no third-party SDKs. We have no mechanism to track, identify, or profile users of any age.

iCloud Sync

If enabled by the device owner, saved stories may sync via the device owner's personal iCloud account. This data is managed entirely by Apple and is not accessible to us.

Data Deletion

All data can be deleted by removing the App from the device. iCloud data can be managed via the device's iCloud settings.

GDPR - Your Rights Under EU Law

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of Access: You can request a copy of all personal data we process about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You can correct any inaccurate personal data directly within the App.
  • Right to Erasure: You can delete all your data by removing the App and clearing iCloud data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Your data is stored locally and in your personal iCloud account, giving you full control and portability.

Legal Basis for Processing: We process your data based on your consent (by choosing to use the App) and the necessity to provide the App's functionality. All data remains on your device and personal iCloud account.

Data Retention: We do not retain any of your data on our servers. Data exists only on your device and in your personal iCloud account for as long as you choose to keep it.

Exercising Your Rights: To exercise any of these rights or if you have questions, contact us at ruslanlutsenko330@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
