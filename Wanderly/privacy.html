Wanderly - Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 7, 2026

Introduction

Wanderly ("the App") is a travel planning application developed by Ruslan Lutsenko. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection & Storage

All trip plans, places, routes, budget data, and photos you enter into the App are stored locally on your device and optionally synced via your personal iCloud account. We do not collect, transmit, or store your data on any external servers.

Location Services

Wanderly may request access to your location for map features, points of interest, and route planning via Apple MapKit. Location data is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers or third parties beyond Apple's MapKit service.

Camera & Photos

The App may request access to your camera and photo library for the photo journal feature. Photos are stored locally on your device and synced only via your personal iCloud account. They are not shared with third parties.

Financial Data

Budget and expense data you enter into the App is stored locally on your device. Your financial information never leaves your device except via your personal iCloud sync. We have no access to your financial data.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit your personal data to any third parties. There are no third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools in the App.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by removing the App from your device. If you use iCloud sync, you can also delete your data from iCloud via your device settings.

GDPR - Your Rights Under EU Law

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of Access: You can request a copy of all personal data we process about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You can correct any inaccurate personal data directly within the App.
  • Right to Erasure: You can delete all your data by removing the App and clearing iCloud data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Your data is stored locally and in your personal iCloud account, giving you full control and portability.

Legal Basis for Processing: We process your data based on your consent (by choosing to use the App) and the necessity to provide the App's functionality. All data remains on your device and personal iCloud account.

Data Retention: We do not retain any of your data on our servers. Data exists only on your device and in your personal iCloud account for as long as you choose to keep it.

Exercising Your Rights: To exercise any of these rights or if you have questions, contact us at ruslanlutsenko330@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
