Cleanly - Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 14, 2026

Introduction

Cleanly ("the App") is a storage and photo cleaner application developed by Ruslan Lutsenko. Your privacy is our top priority. This policy explains how we handle your data — the short version is: everything stays on your device.

Privacy-First, On-Device Processing

Cleanly is designed with a privacy-first architecture. All photo scanning, duplicate detection, similarity analysis, blur detection, and contact scanning happens entirely on your device. No images, contacts, or personal data are ever sent to external servers.

Photo Library Access

Cleanly uses Apple's PhotoKit framework to scan your photo library for duplicates, similar photos, blurry images, screenshots, and large files. This scanning is performed entirely on-device. We never upload, transmit, or access your photos outside of your device.

Contacts Access

Cleanly uses Apple's CNContactStore framework to scan your contacts for duplicates and incomplete entries. All contact processing happens on-device. We never upload, transmit, or access your contacts outside of your device.

Data Storage & Sync

Cleaning history and app preferences are stored locally on your device using SwiftData. If you enable iCloud, this data may sync to your personal iCloud account via CloudKit. We do not operate any external servers and have no access to your synced data.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit your personal data to any third parties. There are no third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools in the App. We do not collect any usage analytics.

Subscriptions

Cleanly uses Apple's StoreKit 2 for in-app subscriptions. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by removing the App from your device. If you use iCloud sync, you can also delete your data from iCloud via your device settings (Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Storage).

GDPR - Your Rights Under EU Law

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of Access: You can request a copy of all personal data we process about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You can correct any inaccurate personal data directly within the App.
  • Right to Erasure: You can delete all your data by removing the App and clearing iCloud data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Your data is stored locally and in your personal iCloud account, giving you full control and portability.

Legal Basis for Processing: We process your data based on your consent (by choosing to use the App) and the necessity to provide the App's functionality. All data remains on your device and personal iCloud account.

Data Retention: We do not retain any of your data on our servers. Data exists only on your device and in your personal iCloud account for as long as you choose to keep it.

Exercising Your Rights: To exercise any of these rights or if you have questions, contact us at ruslanlutsenko330@gmail.com.
